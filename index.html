Dokolo has a total population of 227,235 with access rates varying from 71% in Dokolo TC subcounty to 95% in Batta, Dokolo, Kangai, Okwalongwen subcounties. Safely Managed water source stand at 30% and functional Source per Village stand at 75%. Dokolo has 821 domestic water points and 2 piped schemes which serve a total of 202,842 people – 185,000 in rural areas. 106 water points have been non‐functional for over 5 years and are considered abandoned.
